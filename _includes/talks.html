Parsing with Jison

My first talk at PDXjs was a lightning talk on Parsing with Jison. I compared parsing hex colors in JavaScript using Jison and a hand-written regex-based parser.

Parsing JSON

My second talk at PDXjs was Parsing in JS, a more involved talk about the theory and practice of parsing, comparing and contrasting techniques, and even walking through a JSON parser implementation.

PEG.js & Jison

My third talk at PDXjs was PEG.js & Jison, a talk comparing and contrasting PEG.js with Jison.

JS Curry

My fourth talk at PDXjs was JS Curry, a talk about currying functions in JavaScript, and its pitfalls.
